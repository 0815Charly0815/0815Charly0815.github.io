Privacy Policy

App: PayoffPlan: Loan Calculator
Effective date: July 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the PayoffPlan: Loan Calculator mobile application (the “App”) handles your information. The App’s calculations run entirely on your device. The App displays third-party advertising (Google AdMob), which is described in Section 4.

1. Information We Collect

We, the developer, do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on our own servers. The App has no user accounts and no login. However, the third-party advertising provider (Google AdMob) may collect certain data to serve ads, as described in Section 4.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The values you enter (such as loan amount, interest rate, term, extra payment, property tax, and insurance) are saved only in the App’s local storage on your own device, so your inputs are remembered the next time you open the App. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or to any third party. You can remove it at any time by clearing the App’s storage or uninstalling the App.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data. The only third party that may receive data is our advertising provider, Google AdMob, and only for the purpose of delivering ads (see Section 4).

4. Advertising

The App displays ads through Google AdMob. To serve ads, Google and its advertising partners may collect and process certain information, such as your device’s advertising identifier, IP address (from which approximate location may be derived), and app-usage and interaction data. This may be used for personalized advertising and analytics performed by Google.

You can control ad personalization in your device settings (Settings → Google → Ads) and reset or delete your advertising ID there. For details on how Google uses this data, see Google’s Privacy & Terms. The App does not use any other analytics or tracking software.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 18 and over and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

6. Disclaimer

The App is a financial calculator for estimation purposes only. It does not provide loans, financial services, or financial advice. Results are estimates and may differ from the terms offered by any lender.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
